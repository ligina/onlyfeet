# Participant Definition

- P1/P2: Combined group from early recordings collected by Ziang and another student. Due to battery depletion and partially missing recordings during early data collection, some recordings could not be reliably separated into P1 and P2 afterwards. Therefore, they are merged as P1/P2.
- P3: Separate participant recorded on a different date.
- P4: Separate participant recorded on a different date and used as the final held-out test participant in Stage 2.

Stage 1:
- Train: P1/P2
- Validation: P3

Stage 2:
- Train: P1/P2 + P3
- Test: P4
